Murende Village

A Vision for Real Development in Western Kenya

Who We Are

Murende is a small, remote village in Western Kenya — a place where people are pure, open-hearted, and full of potential. Yet despite this strength, the region has long lacked structured guidance, meaningful work, and a clear vision for development.

The Challenge

Across Western Kenya, thousands of young people grow up with hope, talent, and energy — but without the professional training needed to shape their future. Outside of Murende, where Murende College offers several programs for young learners, there are almost no practical courses that truly equip students with market-ready skills. And even for those who complete basic education, continued guidance and mentorship are often missing.

Our Mission

Murende Village was created to change this reality. We believe that real development requires a rigorous, honest approach: preparing young people with practical skills that directly match the needs of their own communities.

Training in Kenya and the Netherlands

To achieve this, we collaborate with the Netherlands, offering selected students a one-year practical learning program in fields such as healthcare support, plumbing, and basic electrical engineering.

Students first receive foundational training here in Murende. They then continue their hands-on education in the Netherlands, where they learn discipline, work ethics, craftsmanship, and modern techniques. After this intensive year of training abroad, they return home — stronger, skilled, and ready to contribute directly to the development of West Kenya.

Our Commitment

Murende Village invests in young people who want to learn, grow, and build a better future for their communities. We believe that sustainable development begins with people — and in Murende, it begins with you.
