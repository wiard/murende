Murende Village

Murende is a small, remote village in Western Kenya — a place where people are pure, open-hearted, and full of potential. Yet despite this strength, the region has long lacked structured guidance, meaningful work, and a clear vision for development.

Today, thousands of young people in West Kenya grow up with hope, talent, and energy — but without the professional training needed to shape their future. Outside of Murende Village, where is the Murende College that has many programs for young people, there are almost no practical programs that truly equip students with useful skills. Besides that, even those who complete school often need continued support and direction.

Murende Village was created to change this reality. We believe that genuine development requires a rigorous, honest approach: preparing young people with practical abilities that match the needs of their own communities. To achieve this, we collaborate with the Netherlands, offering selected students a one-year practical learning program in fields such as healthcare support, plumbing, and basic electrical engineering.

Students begin with foundational training here in Murende, then continue their hands-on education in the Netherlands, where they learn discipline, work ethics, craftsmanship, and modern techniques. After this intensive training year abroad, they return home — stronger, skilled, and ready to contribute directly to the development of West Kenya.

Murende Village invests in young people who want to learn, grow, and transform their communities through practical knowledge and genuine commitment. Development begins with people — and in Murende, it begins with you.
