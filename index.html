Murende College

Murende College prepares young students in Kenya for a one-year practical learning program in the Netherlands. Our curriculum focuses on essential skills in healthcare support, plumbing, and basic electrical engineering.

Students follow a structured training pathway that builds the knowledge, discipline, and confidence they need to succeed abroad. They learn both technical foundations and social-cultural readiness, ensuring a smooth transition into Dutch vocational programs.

After completing their year of hands-on learning in the Netherlands, students return to Kenya with internationally tested skills, ready to strengthen their communities and contribute directly to local development.

Murende College invests in young people who want to learn, grow, and bring sustainable progress back home.
