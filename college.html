Partner Colleges in Kenya

Murende Village offers a unique international learning pathway that prepares Kenyan students for a one-year practical training program in the Netherlands. This opportunity is open to colleges across Kenya that wish to strengthen their curriculum and invest in the long-term development of their students.

How Kenyan Colleges Can Join

Colleges that want to participate in this program must integrate the full learning pathway into their curriculum from the very beginning of a student's educational journey. This ensures that students build a strong foundation before travelling abroad, and that they are fully prepared for the expectations of practical training in the Netherlands.

The international learning pathway includes:

  • Foundational training in healthcare support, plumbing, or basic electrical engineering
  • Preparation for work culture, discipline, and professional behaviour
  • Technical readiness for hands-on learning in the Netherlands

Support After Returning to Kenya

A key requirement for partner colleges is to provide structured guidance when students return from their year abroad. This reintegration phase is essential for transforming international experience into meaningful, lasting development within Kenya.

Reintegration support includes:

  • Professional mentorship after returning home
  • Social and cultural reintegration guidance
  • A guaranteed work placement to ensure that skills learned abroad are applied directly within the Kenyan economy

By partnering with Murende Village, colleges help create a generation of highly skilled young professionals who return with practical experience, international exposure, and a commitment to building the future of their communities.
